System Overview & Grading Logic

Understanding the relationship between Blackboard, the Faculty Portal, and how grades are calculated.

The Dual-System Mandate

Faculty are required to submit grades in two distinct systems:

  • ✓ Blackboard: Used primarily for ongoing student feedback throughout the term.
  • ✓ Faculty Portal / SIS: The official record for transcripts. This is where final calculation happens.

Automated Calculation

The portal simplifies the math. You enter raw scores (decimals allowed), and the system automatically applies the weightings shown in the chart to generate the final letter grade.
